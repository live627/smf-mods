This adds the following new BB codes to your forum:
[*][font=monospace][nobbc][error]content[/error][/nobbc][/font]
[*][font=monospace][nobbc][warning]content[/warning][/nobbc][/font]
[*][font=monospace][nobbc][okay]content[/okay][/nobbc][/font]
[*][font=monospace][nobbc][info]content[/info][/nobbc][/font]

Theere are no settings and no permissions; everyone can use these bbcodes.
