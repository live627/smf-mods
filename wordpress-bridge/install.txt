Bridge logins between WordPress and SMF.

[font=Times New Roman][color=#cc6633][size=3][b]Introduction:[/b][/size][/color][/font]
[hr]
This will synchronize logins between WordPress and SMF. This applies to all accounts on both the forum and the blog.

[list]
[li]Setup this mod at Administration Center Â» Wordpress Bridge.[/li]
[li]Logins are synchronized with your WordPress site once users log into the forum.[list]
[li]The included WP plugin will redirect users to the forum if they try to register or login to the blog site.[list]
[li]The single file [tt]smf-wp-auth.php[/tt] goes into WP's plugins directory[/li]
[li]It should be activated within the SMF site.[/li]
[/list]
[/li]
[/list]
[/li]
[li]The bridge will automatically create new users to try to keep everything in sync.[/li]
[/list]
